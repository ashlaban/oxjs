Pattern Puzzler

Introduction

Welcome to the world of pattern puzzler, where you will be tasked to recreate a specific pattern; However, there is a catch. You can only paint with a limited set of brushes. Can you figure out how to solve the puzzle and win the ultimate prize?

Gameplay

This is the super demo version. You have one brush available that applies the ternary logic operator sum. (This is basically like the binary xor operator but ternary logic is a little bit more weird than binary.)

Anyways, there are three possible values, cyan, brown and magenta representing -1, 0 and 1 respectively. The current brush performs elementwise ternary addition without carry, meaning that you can toggle the state of the individual cells.

The three levels presented are all clearable, but the last one can be really tedious and requires advanced techniques. The first level can be solved in 1 move, the second in 10 and the third requires many, many moves.
